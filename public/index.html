About

ArtArena is a fun multiplayer drawing and AI-powered recognition game.

Each round, all players draw the same word — an AI model scores each drawing for accuracy.

The most accurate artist wins the round. Best cumulative score wins the game!

How to Play

  1. Enter your name and pick a character
  2. Play solo or create/join a multiplayer room
  3. Draw the given word within 60 seconds
  4. AI scores your drawing — accuracy + effort
  5. After 3 rounds, the leaderboard reveals the winner!
